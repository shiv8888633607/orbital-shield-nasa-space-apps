Our Business Model

Orbital Shield provides a critical "Cleaning-as-a-Service" (OCaaS) for the rapidly growing Low Earth Orbit (LEO) economy. We protect multi-billion dollar satellite constellations from the existential threat of space debris.

1. Predictive Risk Analysis: We use live tracking data and proprietary algorithms to predict high-risk collision zones before they become a threat.

2. On-Demand Debris Removal: Our core technology involves swarms of autonomous collector drones and electrodynamic tethers that can be deployed to safely capture and de-orbit identified threats.

3. Revenue Model: We operate on a B2B subscription model. Satellite operators pay a recurring fee per asset for continuous monitoring, risk analysis, and on-demand cleaning services.

Target Market: Our primary customers are commercial satellite operators (e.g., Starlink, OneWeb, Amazon Kuiper), insurance providers, and national space agencies.
